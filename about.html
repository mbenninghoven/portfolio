It was here that I became exposed to various programming languages and tools such a C# and SQL. I also learned a great deal of troubleshooting on Windows machines which gave me an edge up in knowing two major OS platforms. Now I work for a software company that develops audio encoding software and an advertising platform for digital media sales. Here I work with JavaScript, SQL, PHP, HTML and CSS. My skill level with these various languages is not yet mastered which is what I plan to accomplish within the next year.
